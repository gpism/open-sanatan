## Fun Facts

- Did you know that India celebrates **multiple New Years**? From **Ugadi** to **Gudi Padwa**, and **Baisakhi** to **Diwali**, every region has its unique way of marking a fresh start.
- The lunar and lunisolar calendars used in Indian traditions are some of the most precise and complex in the world.
- Sanatan Dharma emphasizes **sustainability** and living in harmony with nature, long before it became a modern buzzword.
